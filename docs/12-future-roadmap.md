# 12 — Roadmap

Status: Governing sequence summary
Last updated: 2026-07-18

The detailed phase requirements and exit criteria are in [07-build-plan.md](07-build-plan.md). This roadmap exists for prioritization, not as a second plan.

## Now — Architecture reset

- Freeze new campaign registers.
- Establish web-only local-first decisions and documentation.
- Build one IndexedDB adapter and migration path.
- Introduce immutable revisions, dataset/installation/user identity, and typed core entities.

## Next — Canonical operational backbone

- Correct location hierarchy.
- Separate process and task.
- Normalize substances/products/SDS/inventory/use.
- Version exposure limits.
- Establish controls, people, SEGs, and effective-dated memberships.

## Then — Occupational-health spine

- Exposure scenarios.
- Structured qualitative assessments.
- Monitoring priorities and sampling plans.
- Sampling events/samples/results/comparisons.
- Professional interpretations and determinations.
- Controls, program/surveillance applicability, actions, verification, and reassessment.

## Then — Two-installation review exchange

- Versioned manifests and tombstones.
- Integrity and schema validation.
- Dry-run classification and dependency checks.
- Side-by-side conflict resolution.
- Review notes, import history, attribution, idempotency, and rollback.

## Then — Field baseline experience

- Unit baseline workspace.
- Contextual process/task/equipment/chemical/SEG/scenario capture.
- Data gaps and evidence.
- Completeness dashboard and review packet.

## Then — Assurance loop

- Observations, inspections, findings, incidents, investigations, actions, verification, and reassessment linked to operational/exposure context.

## Before daily operational dependency

- Full migration and exchange test matrices.
- Backup/recovery exercises.
- Supported-browser, PWA, offline, quota, accessibility, performance, and print acceptance.
- Real Tifton baseline acceptance and manager review acceptance.

## Deferred modules

Training, complex MOC/PSSR, environmental/waste, permits, broad compliance calendars, contractor suites, advanced analytics, clinical records, automatic cloud sync, and real-time collaboration remain deferred.

They may be reconsidered only through the reactivation gates in [08-scope-boundaries.md](08-scope-boundaries.md).

## Roadmap decision filter

A proposed item moves ahead only if it materially strengthens at least one of:

1. Baseline completeness.
2. Exposure-scenario quality.
3. Professional assessment/determination traceability.
4. Monitoring evidence quality.
5. Safe review exchange.
6. Control/action verification.
7. Migration, recovery, security, accessibility, or operational reliability.

Breadth, page count, or register count is not a roadmap outcome.
